About 소개

I started on the operations floor of a call center — which means I understand the problems before I see the data. Over time I moved into reporting and analytics, where I spent 5 years building the dashboards, scorecards, and reports that teams actually depend on.

Beyond reporting, I've built operational tools from scratch. When our CRM couldn't show caller history, I designed and built an interim CRM using Power Apps, Power Automate, and SharePoint — in 5 weeks. The whole department used it for over a year.

I actively use AI tools as part of my workflow — to move faster, write cleaner queries, and communicate findings more clearly. I think of them as a multiplier, not a shortcut.

I'm fluent in English and Korean, which means I can work across international teams, communicate findings clearly in both languages, and bridge gaps that monolingual analysts can't.

콜센터 운영 현장에서 시작해 데이터가 실제로 어떤 문제를 해결하는지 직접 경험했습니다. CRM이 발신자 이력을 지원하지 않았을 때 Power Apps, Power Automate, SharePoint를 활용해 5주 만에 임시 CRM을 직접 구축했고, 부서 전체가 1년 이상 사용했습니다. AI 도구를 업무 가속화 및 품질 향상을 위한 수단으로 적극 활용하고 있으며, 영어와 한국어 모두 유창하게 구사합니다.

PROJECT 01 — FEATURED Power Apps · Power Automate · SharePoint

Interim CRM Tool — Caller History System

임시 CRM 도구 — 발신자 이력 조회 시스템

⬤ Problem

Agents had no way to view a caller's previous interaction history. Every call started from zero — leading to repeated questions, longer AHT, and frustrated customers. The existing CRM didn't support this, and a new one was still in discussion with IT.

상담사들이 발신자의 이전 상호작용 이력을 조회할 방법이 없었습니다. 매번 처음부터 시작해 AHT 증가, 고객 불만으로 이어졌습니다. IT팀과 새 CRM 도입을 논의 중이었지만 공백이 있었습니다.

⬤ What I Built

In 5 weeks, I designed and built an interim CRM using Power Apps as the front-end, SharePoint as the data layer, and Power Automate for routing and notifications. Agents could instantly pull up a caller's full history mid-call.

5주 만에 Power Apps(프론트엔드), SharePoint(데이터), Power Automate(라우팅·알림)를 활용한 임시 CRM을 설계·구축했습니다. 상담사가 통화 중 발신자 이력을 즉시 조회 가능.

⬤ Impact

Adopted by the whole department and used for over a year — well past its "interim" scope. Bridged a critical operational gap while IT evaluated long-term CRM solutions.

부서 전체가 도입해 1년 이상 사용 — '임시' 범위를 훌쩍 넘어섰습니다. IT팀이 장기 CRM을 검토하는 동안 핵심 운영 공백을 해소했습니다.

⬤ THE STORY BEHIND IT

I wasn't asked to build this. I noticed the gap, raised it with IT, and while the long-term solution was being scoped — I built a working one. That's the kind of problem I look for.

요청받은 게 아니었습니다. 공백을 발견하고 IT팀에 문제를 제기했으며, 장기 해결책이 논의되는 동안 직접 작동하는 솔루션을 만들었습니다.

How I Work 업무 방식

I tend to start with the question behind the question. Before building any report or dashboard, I want to understand what decision it's supposed to support — because a metric that doesn't connect to a decision is just noise.

I'm comfortable working across functions — with operations teams, IT, management, and clients — and I think the ability to translate between technical and non-technical audiences is just as important as the technical skill itself.

When I see a gap, I don't wait for someone to assign it to me. I scope it, propose a solution, and build it — the interim CRM being the clearest example of that.

질문 뒤에 있는 진짜 질문에서 시작합니다. 보고서나 대시보드를 만들기 전에 어떤 의사결정을 지원해야 하는지를 먼저 파악합니다. 운영팀, IT, 경영진, 고객사 등 다양한 부서와 협업하는 데 익숙하며, 기술적 내용을 비기술적 언어로 전달하는 능력이 기술력 자체만큼 중요하다고 생각합니다. 공백을 발견하면 직접 범위를 정하고 해결책을 제안하고 만듭니다.

PRINCIPLE 01
Data should reduce uncertainty, not create it
Every output I build is designed to make a decision easier, not to show off what's technically possible.
PRINCIPLE 02
The best tool is the one people actually use
I've seen great dashboards collect dust because no one understood them. Adoption matters as much as accuracy.
PRINCIPLE 03
If the gap exists, fill it
I don't wait to be asked. When I see something broken or missing, I scope a solution and build it.
